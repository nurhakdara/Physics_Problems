Exercise 4: 
Problem:
A 0.5 kg block slides from height 3.0 m (frictionless).
It collides and sticks to a 1.5 kg block at rest.
Find final speed.

Step 1: Energy (Before Collision)

mgh = (1/2)mv²

gh = (1/2)v²

v = √(2gh)

So:

v = √(2 × 9.8 × 3)

v ≈ 7.67 m/s

Step 2: Momentum (Collision)

m₁v₁ + m₂v₂ = (m₁ + m₂)v_f

Given:

m₁ = 0.5 kg
v₁ = 7.67 m/s
m₂ = 1.5 kg
v₂ = 0

Substitute:

(0.5 × 7.67) = 2v_f

v_f ≈ 1.92 m/s

Final Answer:
v ≈ 1.92 m/s


Collision:
v_f = (m₁v₁) / (m₁ + m₂)
