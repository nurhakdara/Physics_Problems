Exercise 3: 
A pendulum with a length of 1.0 meter is released from an initial angle of 
15 degree. What is the speed of the pendulum bob at the bottom of its swing?

Energy Equation

mgh = (1/2)mv²

Mass cancels:

gh = (1/2)v²

Height Formula:

h = L(1 − cosθ)

Substitute Values:

L = 1.0 m
θ = 15°
cos(15°) ≈ 0.966

h = 1(1 − 0.966) = 0.034 m

Solve for Velocity

9.8 × 0.034 = (1/2)v²

v² = 0.666

v ≈ 0.82 m/s

Shortcut Formula

v = √[2gL(1 − cosθ)]

Final Answer:
v ≈ 0.82 m/s
